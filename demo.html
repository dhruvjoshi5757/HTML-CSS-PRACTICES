SPECIAL PURPOSE MACHINE

Special purpose machine (SPM) is a custom-built machine that is designed to perform a specific task or set of tasks. SPMs are not available off the shelf and are not covered in standard manufacturing programs. They are also called bespoke machines. SPMs are designed to meet an industry's particular needs, ensuring optimal results and improved productivity. They are an alternative to multitasking machines.

SPMs can have a variety of elements, including:

  • Limit switches
  • Automatic job clamping
  • Coolant distributor
  • Anti-friction rotary bushing center
  • Angular feed head
  • Recessing head
  • Milling head
  • Planetary speed reducer
  • Hydraulic slide for longer strokes
  • Rotary index table
